Daily Prompts for Nothing


take count
of the offenses
you’ve committed
to yourself
and forgive all of them at once
then breathe as if
for the first time



ask the objects in your household
‘can you help me?’
ask them, one by one
and wait, genuinely, as if each were just about to answer you
believe they are about to answer you



if you could think beyond categories
what would be on your mind?
language works by categories
so don’t cheat by using words
